## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Howard University with research interests in:

- Computational catalysis
- Density Functional Theory (DFT)
- High-performance computing (HPC)
- Machine learning for materials discovery
- Electrocatalysis and ammonia synthesis

My work combines first-prles electronic structure calculations, data-driven modeling, and software development to accelerate catalyst discovery.
